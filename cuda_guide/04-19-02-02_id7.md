### [4.19.2.2. Direct3D Interoperability](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/04-special-topics#id7)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/04-special-topics/#id7 "Permalink to this headline")

Importing Direct3D[11|12] resources to CUDA is supported for Direct3D11 and Direct3D12. We are only looking at the Direct3D12, for Direct3D11 please refer to the CUDA programming guide 12.9.
