### [5.5.7.9. Classification and Comparison](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/05-appendices#classification-and-comparison)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/05-appendices/#classification-and-comparison "Permalink to this headline")

[CUDA Math API](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-math-api/index.html) for classification and comparison functions are available in both host and device code, except for  `__float128`.

All the following functions have a maximum ULP error of zero.
