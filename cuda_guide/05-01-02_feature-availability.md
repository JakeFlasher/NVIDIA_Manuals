## [5.1.2. Feature Availability](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/05-appendices#feature-availability)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/05-appendices/#feature-availability "Permalink to this headline")

Most compute features introduced with a compute architecture are intended to be available on all subsequent architectures. This is shown in [Table 29](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/05-appendices/#compute-capabilities-table-features-and-technical-specifications-feature-support-per-compute-capability) by the “yes” for availability of a feature on compute capabilities subsequent to its introduction.
