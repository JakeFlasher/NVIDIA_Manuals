### [3.5.1.3. Pipelines](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/03-advanced#pipelines)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/03-advanced/#pipelines "Permalink to this headline")

[Pipelines](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/04-special-topics/pipelines.html#pipelines) are a mechanism for staging work and coordinating multi-buffer producer–consumer patterns, commonly used to overlap compute with [asynchronous data copies](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/04-special-topics/async-copies.html#async-copies). [Section 4.10](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/cuda-programming-guide/04-special-topics/pipelines.html#pipelines) has details and examples of using pipelines in CUDA.
