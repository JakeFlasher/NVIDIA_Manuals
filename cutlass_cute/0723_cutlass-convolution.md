# [CUTLASS Convolution](https://docs.nvidia.com/cutlass/latest/media/docs/cpp#cutlass-convolution)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cutlass/latest/media/docs/cpp/#cutlass-convolution "Permalink to this headline")

Implicit GEMM is the formulation of a convolution operation as a GEMM (generalized matrix-matrix
product). Convolution takes an activation tensor and applies a sliding filter on it to produce an
output tensor.
