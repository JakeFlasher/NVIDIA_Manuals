#### [Spacing and line length](https://docs.nvidia.com/cutlass/latest/media/docs/cpp#spacing-and-line-length)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cutlass/latest/media/docs/cpp/#spacing-and-line-length "Permalink to this headline")

- Use spaces, not tabs.
- Use 2 spaces to indent.
- Use at most 100 characters per line.

(Right-align tensor shape layout comments at column 120.
Please see below.)
Lines longer than 100 characters typically wrap unfavorably
when viewed in Github’s pretty printer.
