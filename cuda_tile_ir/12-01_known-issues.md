## [12.1. Known Issues](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/tile-ir/latest/sections#known-issues)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/tile-ir/latest/sections/#known-issues "Permalink to this headline")

- The programming model is missing a section on a cross-tile block kernel such as split-k.
- The bytecode section does not provide exact encoding of each operation, expect this to
be introduced in a future release.
- The semi-formal memory model section is written but does not provide detailed examples of how to
to utilize it.
- Atomics are currently limited in **Tile IR** and will be expanded in a future release.
