## [3.2. Items](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/tile-ir/latest/sections#items)[](https://docs.nvidia.com/cuda/tile-ir/latest/sections/#items "Permalink to this headline")

An item is a kernel definition or a global variable definition.

```default
<items> ::= <kernel_definition> | <global_variable_definition>
```
